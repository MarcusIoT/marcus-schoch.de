seta

Stratasys challenged me in this course to design a product that shows the capabilities of their latest polyjet 3d printer which is also a impactful product to be not only prototyped, but produced using their printers.
Seta is a toothbrush, that holds toothpaste in a cavaty within itself and therefor aids handicapped and multitasking people, reduces trash due to the ability to refill from bigger containers and is the perfect companion for travel.
